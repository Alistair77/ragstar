# Nimbus Robotics — Employee Handbook

## Remote Work Policy

Nimbus Robotics operates as a remote-first company. All full-time employees may
work from anywhere within their country of employment. Employees are expected to
be reachable during core collaboration hours of 10:00 to 15:00 in their team's
primary timezone.

### Home Office Stipend

Every new full-time employee receives a one-time home office setup stipend of
$1,500 to purchase a desk, chair, monitor, and other ergonomic equipment. This
stipend is available during the first 90 days of employment. Receipts must be
submitted through the Expenses portal.

### Internet and Phone Reimbursement

Remote employees receive a monthly internet and phone reimbursement of $75. This
is paid automatically with your monthly salary and does not require receipts.
Employees working from a co-working space may instead claim up to $300 per month
toward a co-working membership, but cannot claim both.

## Paid Time Off

Full-time employees accrue 20 days of paid vacation per year, plus 10 company
holidays. Vacation days accrue monthly and unused days roll over up to a maximum
of 25 days. Nimbus also offers unlimited sick leave, which does not require a
doctor's note for absences shorter than three consecutive days.

### Parental Leave

Nimbus provides 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave for all new parents,
regardless of gender or how the child joined the family. Parental leave must be
taken within 12 months of the birth or adoption.

## Learning and Development

Each employee has an annual learning budget of $2,000 for courses, books,
conferences, and certifications. Unused learning budget does not roll over to the
next year. Conference travel is reimbursed separately under the Travel Policy and
does not count against the learning budget.
