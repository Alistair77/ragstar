# Nimbus Robotics — Travel and Expense Policy

## General Principles

Spend company money as if it were your own. Nimbus trusts employees to make
reasonable decisions. When in doubt, ask your manager before spending, not after.
All expenses must be submitted within 30 days through the Expenses portal with an
itemized receipt.

## Air Travel

Book economy class for all flights under six hours. For flights longer than six
hours, premium economy is permitted. Business class requires VP approval in
advance. Book at least 14 days ahead when possible to reduce cost.

## Accommodation

Hotels should not exceed $250 per night in most cities, or $350 per night in
high-cost cities such as San Francisco, New York, London, and Tokyo. Employees
may keep the savings if they choose to stay with friends or family, up to $75 per
night, no receipt required.

## Meals

While traveling, employees may expense meals up to a daily limit of $80. Alcohol
is reimbursable only during team events and client dinners, not solo meals.
Tips up to 20% are reimbursable.

## Conferences

Nimbus encourages attending one professional conference per year. Conference
registration and travel are reimbursed under this Travel Policy and do not count
against your annual learning budget. You must share a short write-up or give a
lightning talk to the team within two weeks of returning.

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following are never reimbursable: traffic fines and parking tickets,
in-flight entertainment, personal entertainment such as movies, minibar charges,
and any expense without an itemized receipt over $25.
